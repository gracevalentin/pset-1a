crafting!

crafting is the method by which many items and blocks are made in minecraft. in order to craft something players must move items from their inventory into either the 2x2 crafting grid in your inventory or the 3x3 grid of a crafting table.

above, i provided an example crafting recipe that makes a bookshelf. a complete list of crafting recipes is available if you click on the image.
