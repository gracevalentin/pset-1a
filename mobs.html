mobs!

mobs are living game entities that can interact with players/other mobs. they can be divided into three categories based on how they behave: passive, neutral, and hostile. mobs generally drop items and experience points when killed.

passive mobs

passive mobs are harmless to players and usually will flee if attacked. some examples include pigs, cows, horses, parrots, and villagers.

neutral mobs

neutral mobs will only attack a player when provoked and will have different behavior otherwise. some examples include dolphins, wolves, spiders (when the light level is above 9) and zombie pigmen.

hostile mobs

hostile mobs will attack a player when they are within a certain range. some examples include, creepers, skeletons, witches, and guardians.
