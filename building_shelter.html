how to build a basic shelter

it's important to build a shelter, especially when starting out on a new world. it provides a place to keep all of your things and also protection from monsters and other mobs.

  1. cut down around 3 to 4 trees and use your inventory crafting table to make the logs into wooden planks
  2. find and kill sheep until you have three pieces of wool
  3. build a square/rectangle on the ground that is 4x4 or greater (make sure to leave an 1x2 opening for a door)
  4. build the walls up at least three blocks high and fill in the top
  5. craft a crafting table in your inventory and place it inside your house
  6. with the crafting table, craft a bed using wool and wooden planks and place it inside of your house
  7. with the crafting table, craft a door using wooden planks and place it in the doorway
  8. you now have a sufficient shelter and you can add to it as you collect more materials.
